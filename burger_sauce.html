What to Expect:

This pairs perfectly with any burger you throw at it! It's fairly basic which makes it the perfect sauce to quickly whip up while your burgers cook! Let's dive in!

Ingredients for the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup chopped dill pickles
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Putting it all Together:

  1. Combine all ingridients in a bowl
  2. Serve!

Tips and Tricks of the Perfect Burger Sauce

  • Get creative with it! Play around with different flavors and Ingredients!
