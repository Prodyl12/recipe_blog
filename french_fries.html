French Fries

What to Expect

These will deliver the perfect balance between the crunchy exterior and the soft, moist interior that the french fry demands.

Ingredients for French Fries:

Note: All seasonings are on personal taste. So add as much or as little as you want.

  • 1/2 pound of russet potatoes
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder

Putting it all Together:

  1. Cut potatoes into 1/2 inch slices
  2. Rinse and thouroughly pat dry the potatoes
  3. Place potatoes into a mixing bowl and toss with olive olive and desired seasonings
  4. Lay slices onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  5. Place into oven and bake at 375 for 10 minutes. Flip. Bake an additional 10 or until desired doness.

Tips and Tricks of the Perfect Burger

  • You can dunk the potatoes into an ice bath after slicing to create less starchy potatoes
  • Make sure to thouroughly dry your potatoes to get the outside to crisp up.
