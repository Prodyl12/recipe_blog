What to Expect

Basically imagine ever single one of your taste buds are about to explode with delight. This dish is a very classic and customizable meal perfect for even the pickiest of eaters! Let's get started:

Ingredients for Patties:

  • 1 lb ground beef 80/20
  • 1/2 an onion chopped
  • 1/2 cup of freshly shredded cheddar cheese

Optional Ingredients for Assembly:

  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Burger Sauce!

Putting it all Together:

  1. Preheat grill to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine ground beef, shredded cheese, and chopped onion in a large mixing bowl
  3. Make large sized patties out of the ground beef mixture (don't worry if they're too big because they'll shrink a little during the cooking process) and set aside
  4. Place patties on grill
  5. Grill until internal temp is around 135 degrees fahrenheit

Tips and Tricks of the Perfect Burger

Don't forget to make an indentation into the middle of the burger patty! It will prevent the patties from cooking wrong.
